Detailed Product Information

Craftsmanship

Each Marble Sitting Buddha Statue is individually hand-carved by our master artisans who have inherited traditional stone carving techniques passed down through generations. The process involves:

  • Selection of premium quality white marble blocks
  • Rough shaping using traditional tools
  • Detailed carving of facial features and robe patterns
  • Smoothing and polishing to achieve the perfect finish
  • Quality inspection and packaging

Spiritual Significance

The sitting Buddha posture (Dhyana Mudra) represents meditation, concentration, and the pursuit of enlightenment. This statue is not just a decorative piece but also:

  • A symbol of peace and tranquility
  • Ideal for meditation and mindfulness practices
  • Creates positive energy in living spaces
  • Suitable for both personal and spiritual use

Care & Maintenance

To maintain the beauty of your marble Buddha statue:

  • Clean with a soft, dry cloth regularly
  • For stubborn stains, use mild soap and water
  • Avoid harsh chemicals and abrasive cleaners
  • For outdoor use, apply marble sealer annually
  • Protect from extreme temperature changes
